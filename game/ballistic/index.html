Game Overview – What is Ballistic?

Ballistic is an addictive brick-breaking puzzle game where you shoot colored balls at rotating circular structures to clear matching blocks. The game challenges your precision and timing as you aim carefully to destroy all bricks before they reach the center. Gameplay

Ballistic gameplay combines shooting mechanics with color-matching puzzles where you fire colored balls from the center of a rotating circle. Your goal is to hit bricks on the outer ring that match your ball's color to destroy them. The circular structure constantly rotates, requiring you to time your shots perfectly and predict where matching colors will be. As you clear bricks, the formation moves closer to the center, creating pressure to shoot faster and more accurately. The game features power-ups like multicolor balls that destroy any brick and explosive balls that clear multiple blocks at once. The Ballistic gameplay becomes increasingly intense as rotation speeds increase, color patterns become more complex, and bricks move faster toward the danger zone. Difficulty peaks when you face dense brick formations with fewer color matches, requiring strategic power-up usage and lightning-fast reactions to survive.

How to Play

Press Play to start shooting from the center circle. Aim at bricks on the outer ring that match your ball color. Tap or click to fire your colored ball at the target. Destroy matching bricks to prevent them from reaching the center. Clear all bricks in a section to progress further. Collect power-ups that appear to gain special abilities. Use multicolor balls to destroy any brick regardless of color. Keep shooting accurately as rotation speed increases with each level.
