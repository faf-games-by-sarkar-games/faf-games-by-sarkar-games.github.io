Shell Shockers FAQs

Who created Shell Shockers?

Shell Shockers was created by Blue Wizard Digital, a Canadian game studio known for its fun, quirky online shooters. The game stands out for turning eggs into armed soldiers in a fast-paced, first-person shooter experience.

What is the core gameplay of Shell Shockers?

In Shell Shockers, players control armed eggs in intense multiplayer battles. The goal is to eliminate opponents, survive as long as possible, and climb the leaderboard by using a variety of weapons — all while avoiding getting “cracked.”

What game modes are available in Shell Shockers?

Shell Shockers features several exciting modes: Free For All (FFA): Every egg fights for itself. Teams: Join a team of eggs and compete against the opposing squad. Captula the Spatula: A fun twist on capture-the-flag where you must steal and hold a golden spatula to score points.

How do the controls work in Shell Shockers?

Use standard first-person shooter controls: WASD – Move Mouse – Aim and shoot Spacebar – Jump R – Reload Shift – Aim down sights (for scoped weapons)

How do the weapons and classes work in Shell Shockers?

There are seven egg classes, each with unique weapons and playstyles: Scrambler (Shotgun) – Close-range power. EggK-47 (Assault Rifle) – Balanced, all-around. Free Ranger (Sniper) – Long-range precision. RPEGG (Rocket Launcher) – Explosive attacks. Whipper (SMG) – Fast rate of fire. Crackshot (DMR) – Medium-range accuracy. TriHard (Dual Pistols) – High mobility, quick shots.

Can you customize your egg in Shell Shockers?

Yes! You can customize your egg’s shell color, hat, weapon skin, and melee weapon. These cosmetic options help personalize your egg while you crack your enemies in style.

What are some tips to succeed in Shell Shockers?

Keep moving to avoid enemy fire, and use cover between shots. Learn to aim quickly — headshots deal the most damage. Use the right weapon for each map, manage your ammo carefully, and always reload before engaging in close combat. Mastering movement and timing is key to becoming the ultimate egg warrior! 🥚💥
