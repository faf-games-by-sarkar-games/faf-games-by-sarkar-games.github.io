In Wheely 6, your mission is to help Wheely rescue his beloved after she’s kidnapped by an evil knight. You’ll solve puzzles, overcome obstacles, and explore medieval-themed levels to save the day.

You use your mouse to control the game. Click on Wheely to make him move or stop, and click on levers, buttons, and other interactive objects to manipulate the environment and clear his path.
